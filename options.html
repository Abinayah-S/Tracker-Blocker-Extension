About Privacy Shield
v1.0.1
What is Privacy Shield?

Privacy Shield is a privacy-focused browser extension that blocks known tracking scripts and domains in real-time. It helps protect your privacy by preventing companies from tracking your online activities.

Features
🛡️Blocks known advertising and analytics trackers
📊Real-time statistics of blocked trackers
✅Whitelist domains where trackers should not be blocked
❌Add custom tracking domains to your blacklist
📦Export and import your settings
🔢Badge counter showing blocked requests per tab

Privacy Notice

Your data is stored locally on your device. We never send your browsing data to any external servers. All statistics and settings are kept private.
